Location permission is now contextual: no prompt on launch, only when you tap "Use my location," with recovery to App Settings or the system location panel. Added deletion controls for recent searches (remove one or clear all) and an opt-in "share coarse location only" mode for the weather content provider. The geocoding host is now certificate-pinned to protect typed place names. Fewer false-positive health and pet-pavement alerts on clear days, plus lighter background battery use.
