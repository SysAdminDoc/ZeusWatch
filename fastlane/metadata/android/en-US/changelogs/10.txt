v1.0.0 - Initial public release

- Share weather as text or rendered image
- App shortcuts (long-press launcher icon)
- Deep link support (nimbus:// URI scheme)
- Adaptive layouts for landscape and tablet
- Full TalkBack accessibility on all components
- Predictive back gesture support
- GitHub Actions CI/CD
- F-Droid metadata

Previous versions delivered: current conditions, hourly/daily forecasts, interactive radar, severe weather alerts with notifications, home screen widgets, air quality + pollen data, moon phase, multi-location support, and offline caching.
