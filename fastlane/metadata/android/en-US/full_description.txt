Nimbus Weather is a free, open-source Android weather app with a premium dark interface inspired by The Weather Channel. No API keys, no ads, no tracking.

Features:
- Current conditions with dynamic sky gradients
- 48-hour scrollable hourly forecast
- 16-day expandable daily forecast
- Interactive radar map with RainViewer global tiles
- NWS severe weather alerts with push notifications (US)
- Air quality index with pollutant breakdown
- Pollen levels for 6 species
- Moon phase and astronomy data
- Multiple saved locations
- Home screen widgets (3 sizes)
- Share weather as text or image
- Landscape and tablet adaptive layouts
- Full TalkBack accessibility support

All weather data comes from Open-Meteo (free, no key required). Radar imagery from RainViewer. US severe weather alerts from the National Weather Service.

The app uses a deep navy color palette with glassmorphism effects and animated weather particles. Dark theme only.

Built with Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Hilt, Retrofit, Room, and MapLibre. Targets Android 8.0+ (API 26).
